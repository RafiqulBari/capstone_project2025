Label Semantics for Robust Hyperspectral Image Classification

Rafin Hassan · Zarin Tasnim Roshni · Rafiqul Bari · Alimul Islam

North South University

Abstract Project Page
Paper PDF Slides Poster
Abstract:
Large-scale multi-modal training with image-text pairs imparts strong generalization to CLIP model. Since training on a similar scale for videos is infeasible, recent approaches focus on the effective transfer of image-based CLIP to the video domain. In this pursuit, new parametric modules are added to learn temporal information and inter-frame relationships which require meticulous design efforts. Furthermore, when the resulting models are learned on videos, they tend to overfit on the given task distribution and lack in generalization aspect...

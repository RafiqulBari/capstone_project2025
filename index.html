Label Semantics for Robust Hyperspectral Image Classification

Rafin Hassan · Zarin Tasnim Roshni · Rafiqul Bari · Alimul Islam

North South University

Abstract Project Page
Paper PDF Slides Poster Award
Abstract:
Hyperspectral imaging (HSI) classification is difficult due to high dimensionality and sparse training data, which can lead to overfitting. Most models rely solely on spectral-spatial data. To address this, we propose a semantic spectral-spatial fusion network (S3FN), which integrates class-specific text descriptions generated by LLM. These descriptions are encoded using models such as BERT or RoBERTa to improve feature-label alignment. Evaluated on three benchmark datasets—Hyperspectral Wood, Hyperspectral Blueberry, and DeepHS-Fruit—S3FN significantly improves classification performance. Our results demonstrate the synergy between text semantics and spectral-spatial data, advancing semantically enhanced HSI classification models.
